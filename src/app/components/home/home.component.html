  • Crop and soil monitoring – companies are using Computer Vision and Deep Learning algorithms to process data captured by drones and/or software-based technology to monitor crop and soil health.
  • Predictive analytics – Machine Learning models are being developed to track and predict various environmental impacts on crop yield, such as weather changes.

Energy and mining for the future.

the energy industry will begin to change in the coming years and the intervention of the human being will be less and less.
